CONSTILLATIONS
ARIES

Aries is one of the constellations of the zodiac that is located in the northern celestial hemisphere between Pisces to the west and Taurus to the east.

CANCER

Cancer is one out of twelve constellations of the zodiac. Cancer is a medium-size constellation and it's stars are rather faint.

CAPRICORN

Capricorn is the tenth astrological sign in the zodiac out of twelve total zodiac signs, originating from the constellation of Capricornus, the horned goat.

LIBRA

Libra Is the seventh astrological sign in the Zodiac. The Sun transits this sign on average between (northern autumnal equinox) September 23 and October 22.
